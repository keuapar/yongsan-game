Approach him

You’re not sure exactly what to say to him, so you end up just kind of standing awkwardly in front of him.

“Yes?” he asks. “Is there something you need? You look a little bit shaken. Is everything okay?”

He looks slightly less suspicious than the last guy, but you’re not sure. Is this all a ploy? The top two buttons of his uniform are undone and you see that he is wearing a cross. Does that make him more credible?

“I’m John,” he says after you’ve been silent for awhile. “What’s your name?”

It seems like a name for a very harmless guy, right? Do you remember any bad guys named John? You think back to the clues you’ve discovered along the way

and wonder if you should
