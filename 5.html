Explore the room more

There has to be a way to escape and you won’t get those answers by rushing ahead. You decide to look around the room a little more. You go back to the desk and search in the darkness.

A lamp!

You turn it on and as your eyes adjust to the light, you gasp. The table held more than one set of scratch marks. It was littered all over with it.

“DON’T TRUST ANYONE.” “HE HAS EYES AND EARS EVERYWHERE.”

The room is sparse aside from the table and the spilt bottles of smelling salts.

There isn’t anything else here so you turn off the light

and decide to

test the door!
