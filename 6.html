You wake one of the girls

You decide to wake the girl closest to you. Please be nice, you hope. You gently shake her shoulder as she rouses awake.

She jolts awake and her eyes are wide with fear as she looks at you. Instantaneously, the fear is replaced by a guarded stare.

“Hello? Where are we? What is this place?”

She gives you a sad smile as she says, “If you’re lucky, they’ll at least send you to a labour camp. Otherwise, you’re going to be warming a lot of men’s beds.”

You feel like you can’t breathe. You figured you were in a bad place, but you were silently holding out hope that maybe it wasn’t as bad as it looked. You can’t seem to formulate a response, so you just sit there, shaking.

“What will you do now?” she asks.

"I don't know..."
