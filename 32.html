“There isn’t much I can do then, I don’t want to keep difficult girls anyway. You’re free to leave,” she says dismissing you with a flick of her wrist, all pretense of warmth fading away.

You are stunned by this sudden change and slowly get up and head out the door.

“But,” she calls as you open the door. “Be careful.”

It is an ominous warning and you feel a little more afraid. You walk back down towards the gate. The way is confusing and you have to back track a few times before you find the exit. When you do find the gate, you see a man casually leaning against it.

“Hello, girl,” he says as he sees you coming. “I think its time for you to come back.”

There is no other escape in this compound.
