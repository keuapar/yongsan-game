The main door

You creep to the door at the end of the corridor and try to open it, but it is locked. There is another room next to the door, so you try to enter it.

Luckily, that door isn’t locked. You peer inside and pray no one is there. And to your surprise, there really isn’t anyone around. You don’t know how long that will be true for, so you have to act quickly.

The room looks like an office with a large desk in the centre, messy with papers and smelling like cigarettes and beer. You walk over to the desk and rifle through hoping to find a key.

There are ledgers and reports strewn across the desk.

Do you take the time to read them?
