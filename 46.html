You choose to trust her

She doesn’t seem like a bad person, but what do you know. You don’t even know where you are. You just hope that it wasn’t a bad choice to trust her.

You walk in after her and she locks the door behind you.

Uh-oh. Maybe this was a mistake after all. You quickly scan the room for other means of escape. Perhaps there is another door in the kitchen at the back of the room. Or could you overpower her. Let’s see, the tables seem heavier than you can lift, but there are stools. You could definitely throw those at her.

She moves to the kitchen and puts a pot on the stove. “Take a seat.”

You’re not sure if you should. But as the pot starts heating up, the homely smell of fish and spices, eases a bit of the tension in your body. You decide to sit.

“You are a long, long way from home.

Sit and eat.”
