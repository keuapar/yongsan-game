Uncertainty and Fear

You feel paralysed by fear. Your scattered thoughts cannot seem to coalesce. There doesn’t seem to be any hope.

“Hey, calm down,” she says laying a gentle hand on your shoulder.

Only then did you notice that you were shaking violently. You take a breath, one… two… three…

“How will we ever get out of here?” you ask, on the verge of tears.

She looks at you sadly,

and says

"There might be a way."
