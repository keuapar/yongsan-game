Daegutang shop owner

Owner of Won Daegutang started his/her business since 1979. His/Her restaurant is popular with local workers and the soldiers from the nearby army base that soon attract other codfish restaurants gathered nearby and formed Daegutang Alley. The codfish hotpot is filled with large chunks of codfish and intestines in a spicy broth balanced with water parsley and bean sprouts.

Park so geum

Park so geum (1914 - 1965) was a full-time painter and one of the most influential modernist. Although he didn’t formally receive art education in school, his skills won him numerous prizes. He predominantly used light gray color, to depict simple yet Korea-like themes in folklore sense.

An artist

In 1960s, many artists gathered around Samgakji as the demand for ‘barbershop painting' boomed. The mass produced commercial paintings are exported to foreign markets, and artists earned money to open studios and shops. However, it did not last long as commercial arts in China and South East Asia were sold at lower prices. Exports of domestic art have declined sharply, most domestic export companies have closed, and many artists also lose their market and moved away.
