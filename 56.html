Yes, I trust him

You stay silent for a moment as you try to formulate what you will ask him, as he continues to paint you. Oddly, you feel calm watching him work and in no time he’s finished and turns his easel around to show you the final product.

And it is beautiful. He perfectly captures the look of the autumn leaves under the orange glow of the street lights. His depiction of you casts you as serene and contemplative.

“Can I come with you,” you ask.

He seems surprised at your question. “I don’t have much in directions or means. Will that be a problem?”

You shake your head and wonder if you should share your situation with him, but decide against it. It should be fine.

“Then, I am happy to have a travelling partner,”

he smiles.
